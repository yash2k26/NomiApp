## KEY RULES FOR DELIVERY

1. **Present tense ONLY** — "we built", "we deliver", never "we hope to" or "we're trying to"
2. **Re-hook every slide** — each transition should make judges want to hear the next thing
3. **Don't rush the ending** — pause after "Your companion is waiting" before "Thank you"
4. **Speak with conviction** — you SHIPPED this. Own it.
5. **Time yourself** — if over 4 minutes, cut words from slides 4, 6, 7 (they're the most trimmable)
6. **Record 2-3 takes** — pick the one where you sound most confident, not most polished
