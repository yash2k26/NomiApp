# NOMI Demo Script v2

**Target time:** ~2:30
**Format:** Screen recording + ElevenLabs voiceover, combined in editing
**Vibe:** Showing a friend something cool you built. Casual, a little funny, confident.

---

## Workflow

1. Copy the **Voiceover Script** at the bottom into ElevenLabs
2. Generate audio in one shot (or split at each `[PAUSE]` if timing is tricky)
3. Screen record the app in one continuous take — just do the actions, no talking
4. Drop both into CapCut / DaVinci / iMovie, align audio to screen, trim gaps
5. Done

---

## Section Breakdown (what to show + what the audio says)

### [0:00–0:12] Wallet Connect + Mint

**Screen:** App opens. Tap connect. Phantom opens. Approve. Mint screen appears. Tap mint. Transaction goes through. Pet appears breathing.

**Audio:** "So you open the app, connect your wallet — Phantom, Solflare, whatever. No email, no password. Just your wallet. Then you mint your pet. 0.01 SOL. That's a real NFT on Solana mainnet. And look — there it is. Breathing. Just vibing. It has no idea what's coming."

---

### [0:12–0:28] Home Screen, Stats & Feeding

**Screen:** Show hunger, happiness, energy bars (one is low, pet looks moody). Tap feed. Stat bar goes up. XP floats. Pet does excited animation.

**Audio:** "Three stats — hunger, happiness, energy. They tick down over time, even when the app is closed. Ghost your pet for a day and it knows. It gets sad. It gives you this look.

Let's feed it. Pick a snack, tap, and — see that? Stats go up, XP pops, little guy loses his mind. Each action costs stamina and has a cooldown so you can't just spam it. You gotta come back. Which is exactly the point."

---

### [0:28–0:35] Double Tap Easter Egg

**Screen:** Double-tap pet. It falls over. Gets back up.

**Audio:** "Oh and if you double tap it... it just eats it. Face first. Gets right back up like nothing happened. I don't know why this is everyone's favorite thing but it is."

---

### [0:35–0:55] Shop & Headphones Dance

**Screen:** Shop tab. Browse briefly. Equip headphones. Back to Home. Pet dancing with music.

**Audio:** "There's a whole accessory store — hats, animations, everything priced in SOL. Let's grab headphones. Equip them, go back home, and...

yeah. It dances. Music and everything."

*(3–4 seconds silence — let the dance breathe)*

"First time I got this working I just sat there grinning like an idiot."

---

### [0:55–1:10] Mini Games

**Screen:** Games tab. Open Quick Tap or Memory Match. Play a few seconds. Results screen with XP.

**Audio:** "Six mini games — memory match, quick tap, pattern recall, math rush, couple others. They cost stamina but earn you XP. Some are locked behind levels. It's the kind of thing that makes you open the app one more time before bed. You know how that goes."

---

### [1:10–1:25] Spin Wheel, Adventures & Quests

**Screen:** Spin wheel — spin it, watch reward. Flash adventure zones. Show daily quests.

**Audio:** "Daily spin wheel — free spins, XP, stamina refills, evolution shards. There's adventures too — pick a zone, send your pet off, it comes back hours later with loot. Your pet goes on field trips while you're at work.

And daily quests. Feed 3 times, play twice, that kind of thing. Knock them out for bonus XP."

---

### [1:25–1:40] Diary Entries

**Screen:** Tap diary. Scroll through entries slowly. Let text be readable.

**Audio:** "Ok this is the part that gets people. When you close the app, your pet writes diary entries. What it did while you were gone. What it was thinking about. Some are funny. Some are weirdly sweet. And some will straight up guilt trip you for not checking in. It's a fake pet with real emotional range. I don't make the rules."

---

### [1:40–2:00] Leveling, Personality & Profile

**Screen:** Show level-up modal or achievement. Show personality traits. Show share card.

**Audio:** "50 levels, five evolution stages. Each milestone unlocks real perks — shorter cooldowns, shop discounts, bonus stamina. Your pet develops personality based on how you treat it. Feed it a lot, it becomes a foodie. Send it on adventures, it gets brave. Every pet ends up different.

And there's a share card. Because what's the point of raising a pet if you can't flex on people a little."

---

### [2:00–2:20] On-Chain Proof & Close

**Screen:** Profile tab. Tap wallet address — Solscan opens in browser showing real transactions. Back to home. Pet breathing or dancing. Dialogue bubble pops up.

**Audio:** "Last thing. Everything is on-chain. That's your real wallet address — tap it, opens on Solscan. Every purchase stored as a transaction memo. No backend. No server. No database. Delete the app, reinstall, everything restores from the blockchain. The chain is the database.

That's NOMI. Built by one dev. Shipping on the Solana dApp Store. Your pet's waiting."

---

## Recording Checklist

- [ ] Pet at level 5+ so features are visible
- [ ] Hunger and happiness NOT maxed (leave room to show feeding)
- [ ] Headphones owned but NOT equipped (so you can equip on camera)
- [ ] At least 1 free spin available
- [ ] Diary entry exists (close app for 2+ hours beforehand)
- [ ] Stamina available for actions and games
- [ ] Daily quests not completed yet
