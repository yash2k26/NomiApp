# NOMI Demo Video Script

**Total time: ~2:00**
**Format: Screen recording + voiceover. No background music.**

---

## SETUP (before you hit record)

- Fresh wallet connect ready (or already connected)
- Pet at level 5+ so features are visible
- Hunger and happiness NOT maxed (so you can feed)
- Headphones owned but NOT equipped
- At least 1 free spin available
- Diary entry unread
- Stamina available

---

## THE SCRIPT

### [0:00-0:12] Wallet Connect & Minting

**Show:** App opens to wallet connect screen. Tap connect. Phantom/Solflare opens. Approve. Then the mint screen appears. Tap mint. NFT mints. Pet appears on screen for the first time, breathing softly.

**Say:** "Alright so, you open NOMI, connect your Solana wallet, and the first thing it asks you to do is mint your pet. That's a real NFT on Solana mainnet, by the way."

---

### [0:12-0:25] Meet Your Pet & Stats

**Show:** Pet is breathing on home screen. Camera lingers on the 3D model. Then pan down to show hunger, happiness, and energy bars. Maybe one of them is low and NOMI looks a little moody.

**Say:** "And here it is. Your NOMI. It breathes, it reacts, it has moods. See these three bars? Hunger, happiness, energy. They're all ticking down in real time, even when you're not looking. Close the app for a few hours and come back to a very passive-aggressive little creature who definitely noticed you were gone."

---

### [0:25-0:38] Feeding & Care

**Show:** Tap Feed button. Pick a food variant. Stat bar animates upward. XP floats up. Pet does the excited jump animation. Show the level/XP progress.

**Say:** "But you can fix that. Tap feed, pick something, and watch it light up. That little XP number floating up? That's real progression. There are 50 levels in this game and five full evolution stages, so your NOMI literally grows up with you. Every time you feed it, play with it, or just check in, you're building something together."

---

### [0:38-0:48] Double Tap Easter Egg

**Show:** Double-tap the pet. It does the fall-over animation. Laughing sound effect plays. Pet gets back up.

**Say:** "Oh and if you double tap it, it just... falls over. Flat on its face. It's not hurt, it thinks it's hilarious actually. Gets right back up like nothing happened. I don't know why this is everyone's favorite feature but it is."

---

### [0:48-1:05] Shop & Headphones = Dance (THE WOW MOMENT)

**Show:** Go to Shop tab. Browse the accessories for a second. Buy/equip headphones. Go back to Home. Pet is now wearing headphones and DANCING. Music starts playing.

**Say:** "Now let's go shopping. There's a whole accessory store, everything priced in SOL, and it all goes on-chain as transaction memos. So grab some headphones, put them on your NOMI, go back home, and... yeah. It dances. Full-on vibing with music and everything. I'm not going to lie, the first time I saw this working I just sat there smiling like an idiot for about five minutes."

> Let the dance play for 3-4 seconds here. No talking. Just let it breathe.

---

### [1:05-1:18] Diary Entries

**Show:** Tap the diary button. Scroll through one or two entries slowly. Let the text be readable.

**Say:** "Here's the part that gets people. When you close the app, your NOMI writes diary entries. About its day. About what it did while you were gone. What it was thinking about. Some of them are funny, some of them are surprisingly sweet, and honestly some of them will make you feel a little guilty for not checking in sooner. It's a fake pet with real emotional range."

---

### [1:18-1:28] Spin Wheel & Mini Games

**Show:** Games tab. Open spin wheel. Spin it. Watch it land on a reward. Then quickly flash into a mini-game like Quick Tap or Memory Match. Show a few seconds of gameplay. Exit with results screen.

**Say:** "There's also a daily spin wheel with XP, evolution shards, and stamina refills. And six mini-games that cost stamina to play but earn you XP. It's the kind of stuff that makes you open the app one more time before bed, just to squeeze in one more round."

---

### [1:28-1:42] On-Chain Proof

**Show:** Profile tab. Show NFT mint address. Tap the Solscan link and it opens in browser. Show SOL balance. Scroll through transaction history with real transactions.

**Say:** "And everything, I mean everything, is on-chain. That's your actual NFT mint address. Tap it and it opens on Solscan. Every purchase, every accessory, every premium upgrade is stored as a transaction memo on Solana. There's no backend. No server. No database. If you delete the app and reinstall it, your entire pet, your progress, your purchases, it all restores from the blockchain. The chain IS the database."

---

### [1:42-1:52] Share Card

**Show:** Scroll down to the share card. Beautiful dark gradient card with circular stat gauges, pet name, level, on-chain info. Tap share.

**Say:** "And when you want to show it off, there's a share card. Your pet's name, stats, on-chain info, all in one clean image. Because honestly, what's the point of raising a pet if you can't brag about it a little?"

---

### [1:52-2:00] End

**Show:** Back to home. Pet breathing or dancing. Dialogue bubble appears with something sweet.

**Say:** "This is NOMI. A companion that lives on Solana, grows with you, and genuinely misses you when you're not around. Built by one developer. Coming to your Seeker soon."

---

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Practice 3 times** before recording so transitions are smooth
2. **Pre-own headphones** so you just tap equip, no purchase flow needed
3. **Record screen and voice separately** if possible, it's way easier to get the pacing right
4. **Let each animation finish** before moving on
5. **Headphones-to-dance is your hero moment** — let the silence after it do the work
6. **Diary is your emotional moment** — slow down, let judges actually read the entries
7. **Share card is your visual closer** — hold it 3-4 seconds
8. **Talk like you're showing a friend** — present tense, casual, no "um" but also no robot voice
